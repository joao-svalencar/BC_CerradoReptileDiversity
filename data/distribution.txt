I checked the species listed in the SALVE system and compared the list with that provided in the Reptile Database for Squamata species occurring in Brazil. 

Three species included in SALVE are not regarded as occurring in Brazil according to the Reptile Database:

- Apostolepis ambiniger: removed from our analyses following Entiauspe-Neto et al (2021)
- Philodryas patagoniensis: removed from our analyses following Di Pietro et al. (2026)

- Neusticurus medemi: kept in our analyses following Marques-Souza et al (2018)
- Plica plica: kept in our analyses following Ribeiro-Junior and Amaral (2016)
